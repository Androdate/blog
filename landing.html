Androdate

Androdate aims to bring latest version of android to the Indian sub-continent
and also to spread awarness about the latest releases.

What is Androdate?

Androdate is a group of custom-ROM developers who aims to update the phones which are lagged behind because of the lack of support given by the OEM ( Original Equipment Manufacturers. We, as a team is taking a step forward to eliminate the problem to the maximum extent. Most of the OEM’s in India ignore the updates for their end users, nor they do not have awareness about the latest Android releases.

Why Androdate?

Why Androdate? Good question, Androdate looks forward to providing latest Android updates as a custom ROM to the maximum possible and supported smartphones. Phones other than Google made phones ( Nexus, Pixel, and Android One phones) don’t usually get security updates which make the phones more vulnerable. Androdate developers promise to release the main releases and security updates as soon as possible making the phone more secure and up-to-date. Now, you don’t have to worry about having an old android phone.

Our mission

Androdate’s mission is to provide high quality and optimized software enhancing and empowering the Android smartphone using our custom ROM. We also offer latest security releases, patches, and main versions. Only 0.4% of Android smartphones run on the latest version, i.e., Android 7.0 (Nougat). Androdate developers stand forward to make the phones more secure and adding the features, therefore, making it more user-friendly.

Our vision

Most of the Android smartphones rarely gets updated. We target the major part of the Indian sub-continent Android users, who rarely get informed about the various android sweet desserts that they serve. Enlightening the people about the all these will get more power to the end users. This also resolves the biggest problem for the developers, which is targeting the old devices. By updating the most of the smartphones to the latest Android solves the confusion of targeting which version of users. This makes the app to reduce some features and cut down versions. This is what we aim to clear out, wish to contribute us? Read out How to be a part of our team.
